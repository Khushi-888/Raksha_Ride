## 🚀 How to Start (Super Easy!)

### Step 1: Open Your Browser
```
Type this in address bar:

```

### Step 2: You'll See
- Beautiful homepage with yellow and blue colors
- "Enroll as Driver" button

### Step 3: Click "Enroll as Driver"
- Opens registration page
- See 3 steps: Role → Details → Confirm

---

## 👤 For Vehicle Owners

### What You Do:
1. **Select "Vehicle Owner"** - Click the card with 👤 icon
2. **Fill Your Details** - Name, age, mobile, email, etc.
3. **Confirm** - Check everything is correct
4. **Upload Documents** - 5 documents needed:
   - Aadhaar Card (Front)
   - Aadhaar Card (Back)
   - Driving License
   - RC Book
   - Your Selfie
5. **Submit** - Done! Check your email

### What Happens Next:
- ✉️ You get confirmation email
- ⏳ Admin checks your documents
- 🔑 You get login details via email
- ✅ You can start driving!

---

## 🤝 For Rent Drivers

### What You Do:
1. **Select "Rent Driver"** - Click the card with 🤝 icon
2. **Fill Your Details** - Name, age, mobile, email, etc.
3. **Enter Owner Info** - Owner's email and password
4. **Submit** - Done!

### What Happens Next:
- 📧 Owner gets email with your details
- 👀 Owner sees your information
- ✅ Owner approves (or rejects)
- 📄 Owner uploads vehicle documents
- ✉️ Both get confirmation emails
- ⏳ Admin checks documents
- 🔑 Both get login details
- ✅ Both can start!

## 💡 Simple Explanations

### What is "Owner Driver"?
- You own the vehicle
- You drive it yourself
- You upload your own documents

### What is "Rent Driver"?
- You don't own the vehicle
- You rent it from someone
- Owner uploads vehicle documents
- You can see documents but not change them

### What is "Light/Dark Mode"?
- Two color themes
- Light = White background
- Dark = Dark background
- Switch anytime with one click

### What are "Animations"?
- Things that move smoothly
- Cards that lift when you hover
- Icons that bounce gently
- Smooth color changes
