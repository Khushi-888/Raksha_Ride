🔄 Registration Flows

👤 Owner Driver Flow
Step 1: Select "Vehicle Owner" role
Step 2: Fill all your details (name, age, mobile, email, etc.)
Step 3: Confirm your information
Step 4: Upload 5 documents (Aadhaar front/back, License, RC, Selfie)
Step 5: Receive email confirmation
Step 6: Admin verifies your documents
Step 7: Get login credentials via email ✓ Complete!
🤝 Rent Driver Flow
Step 1: Select "Rent Driver" role
Step 2: Fill your details + owner's email and password
Step 3: Submit registration
Step 4: Owner receives email with confirmation link
Step 5: Owner clicks link and sees your details
Step 6: Owner approves your request
Step 7: Owner uploads vehicle documents
Step 8: Both receive email confirmation
Step 9: Admin verifies documents
Step 10: Both get login credentials ✓ Complete!
